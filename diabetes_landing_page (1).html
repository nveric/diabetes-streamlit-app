Diabetes Risk Predictor

This tool helps you assess the likelihood of developing diabetes based on clinical indicators like glucose level, BMI, blood pressure, age, and more. It's powered by a machine learning model trained on real patient data.

How It Works

  • Enter basic health indicators (glucose, BMI, etc.).
  • Our model predicts the risk using a Random Forest Classifier.
  • Receive a risk category (Low or High) with a confidence score.

Machine Learning Model

  • Dataset: Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset
  • Features: Pregnancies, Glucose, BloodPressure, SkinThickness, Insulin, BMI, DiabetesPedigreeFunction, Age
  • Model: Random Forest Classifier
  • Accuracy: ~87%

* This app is for educational/demo purposes only and not a substitute for professional medical advice.
